Riding School

General Information

The riding lessons are based on the type (private/semi private) as well as your riding level. We follow the Canadia rider level program found here.

Lessons are available for both children and adults. Children under 5 would enroll in the "tiny riders program" which caters more to their age and physical capacities.

Helmets are provided if you don't have your own. You must come in shoes that have at least a half-inch heel in order to be able to ride. We have some spare riding boots but we cannot guarantee that we will have your size. Full-length pants are strongy recommended.

Our programmes are suitable for both adults and children, those who want to compete or those who just want to have fun. Our coaches will adjust to your needs.
