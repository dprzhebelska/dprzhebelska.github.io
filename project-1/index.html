Ironwood Stables

About Us

We are a small team of coaches and horses dedicated to making horseback riding more accessible to those looking for a more relaxed experience or with busy schedules.

Our programmes are suitable for both adults and children, those who want to compete or those who just want to have fun. Our coaches will adjust to your needs.

Our Services

Riding School

Our riding school is open to all ages, skill levels and ambitions. We specialize in all three major english riding disciplines, dressage, jumpers and hunters to develop a well rounded rider.

Boarding

Our facilities include a heated and cooled tack up area, an infrared heated medium-sized indoor arena, a large outdoor arena with a full set of jumps, 5 paddocks and more for the comfort of your horse.

Read more

Leasing

We offer some horses for half leases to students who want to advance in their riding career. Leasing is a great way to develop a bond with a horse and is a natural step forward in owning your own horse.
